Scary Movie (2000) – Review, Plot, Cast & Viewing Guide

Scary Movie (2000)

Released in 2000, Scary Movie is a comedy parody that pokes fun at some of the most popular horror films of the 1990s. Packed with outrageous humor, unexpected twists, and memorable characters, it became one of the highest-grossing parody films of its time.

Plot Overview

The story follows a group of teenagers whose lives take a bizarre turn after a mysterious incident. As strange events begin to unfold, they find themselves caught in a series of hilarious situations inspired by famous horror movies. Instead of delivering scares, the film focuses on over-the-top comedy and clever spoofs.
