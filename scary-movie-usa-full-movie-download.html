🎬 Scary Movie (2000)

Overview

Scary Movie is a comedy parody film released in 2000. It humorously spoofs several popular horror movies and became one of the best-known parody films of its era.

Plot

A group of teenagers becomes caught up in a series of bizarre events that parody famous horror films. Instead of scares, the movie focuses on fast-paced comedy and exaggerated humor.
